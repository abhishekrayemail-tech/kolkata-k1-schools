Typical Pre‑Primary Admissions Cycle

Several high‑demand schools show date‑bound windows (often mid‑year to early autumn). Some schools describe rolling admissions (e.g. CIS). Birla High School's 2026–27 forms opened Jun 26 – Aug 3, 2025; La Martiniere's Lower Nursery forms were posted in July 2025.

Jun–Jul

Registration / forms open. South Point (25 Jun–9 Jul 2025), Birla High (Jun 26), La Martiniere (Jul). Schools display explicit windows and "closed" banners after deadlines.

Aug–Sep

Form submission + doc verification. Birla High deadline Aug 3. Sushila Birla forms till Aug 13. Interaction sessions begin.

Aug–Oct

Interaction / observation (child + parent). Birla High interaction lists posted Aug 5. SBGS interaction Aug 30–Sep 1.

Sep–Nov

Selected / waitlist publication + fee deposits. SBGS final list Sep 15. BSS School merit lists published.

Dec–Feb

Late vacancies; sibling‑staff transfer seats; relocation opens. Shri Shikshayatan forms remain active.

Mar–Apr

Session starts. Birla High: Apr 1, 2026. Orientation & settling‑in period for most schools.

Preparation

Admission Checklist for Nursery / LKG

📄 Child Documents

  • Birth certificate (original + 2–3 self‑attested copies)
  • Passport photos (child + parents) — 8 child + 4 each parent (some schools like G.D. Goenka require this)
  • Immunisation record (if requested)
  • Blood group report of child (required by Birla High, many others)
  • Aadhaar card of child (mandatory for Heritage School and many others)
  • Previous school record / TC if applicable

👤 Parent / Guardian Documents

  • Address proof (Aadhaar / utility bill / rent agreement / voter ID)
  • ID proof — Aadhaar cards of both parents (mandatory at most schools)
  • Educational qualification certificates of both parents (Birla High, SBGS require)
  • Income proof — IT return / Form 16 / salary slip (Birla High, G.D. Goenka)
  • PAN card of both parents (some schools like The Newtown School explicitly require)
  • Caste certificate if applicable (SC/ST/OBC)
  • Medical certificate in prescribed format (La Martiniere requires from registered practitioner)
  • Court/legal guardianship papers if applicable (adoption, single parent: separation/custody decree)

🎯 School‑Specific Prep

  • Fill online form early — Birla High uses admissiontree.in; many schools have own portals
  • Confirm "entry class" mapping: Playgroup / Lower Nursery / Upper Nursery / LKG / Balvatika — and the school's age cut-off date (31 Mar vs 1 Apr vs 1 Jan varies by school)
  • Note: both parents must attend interaction at most schools (SBGS, Birla, La Martiniere)
  • Carry original documents for verification at interaction — many schools won't accept screenshots
  • Watch for explicit "closed" banners — some sites display registration windows and close dates prominently
  • Plan commute trial at peak school hours (7:30–8:30 AM)
  • Track multiple school deadlines — set calendar reminders for Jun–Aug windows
Inclusion & SEN

SEN / Speech‑Support Questions to Ask Schools

Ask these in writing (email) so you have a record. Heritage School notably requires parents to disclose suspected/identified special needs at admission for support planning.

🔍 Screening & Support Model
  • Do you have an on‑staff special educator / SEN coordinator / counsellor? How many, and how are they allocated across sections?
  • What is the support model in KG: push‑in support, pull‑out sessions, small‑group language work, or referrals only?
  • Do you run structured early‑years language development programmes (phonological awareness, vocabulary building, storytelling)? Or is speech/language support referral‑only to external therapists?
🗣️ Speech & Developmental Needs
  • If a child has mild speech delay, what accommodations are typical (extra response time, visual supports, simplified instructions, peer‑buddying)?
  • Can parents bring external reports (SLP / OT / developmental paediatrician) and collaborate on an Individualised Education Plan?
  • Can parents share speech therapy notes, and will the class teacher coordinate routines (e.g. practising target sounds, visual schedules)?
👩‍🏫 Classroom Staffing & Ratios
  • In Nursery/LKG, how many adults are in the room (lead teacher + assistant + floating staff)? Heritage School assigns a 'didi' companion for Nursery/KG.
  • Average class size in Nursery/LKG, and how the school handles high‑needs days (substitutes, co‑teaching)?
  • If special educators exist in the organisation, can they support a child in Nursery/LKG — and how often (daily, weekly, on‑request)?
🤝 Inclusion Policies
  • What is the school's policy on shadow teachers (if needed)? Who hires/trains/supervises them?
  • How are behavioural needs managed (positive behaviour support, sensory supports, quiet corner, movement breaks, counsellor involvement)?
💬 Communication with Parents
  • How frequently do teachers communicate developmental progress (monthly notes, termly meetings, digital logs)?
  • In the first 90 days, will the teacher provide weekly settling-in updates, or only at standard termly PTMs?
  • What is the escalation pathway if a child is struggling (teacher → coordinator → counsellor / SEN lead)?
🏠 Transition & Settling‑In
  • Is there a staggered start for young children?
  • What settling routine is used for separation anxiety?
💰 Fees & Transparency
  • Request the latest fee sheet in writing — clarify whether one‑time deposits are refundable (several schools publish one‑time and recurring schedules separately).
  • Are there additional charges for SEN support, shadow teacher supervision, or pull‑out sessions?
  • What is the fee revision frequency and notice period?

Methodology & Source Policy

Compiled as of 24–25 Mar 2026. Enhanced edition adds web-researched data from school official sites, admission portals, CISCE school locator expansion, and reputable aggregators.

Primary / Official Sources

  • School official websites (admission notices, fee tables, contact pages, early-years programme descriptions)
  • CBSE SARAS affiliation records and mandatory public disclosure documents (staffing, ratios, board results)
  • CISCE school locator — used to expand directory with 30+ ICSE/ISC schools and official contact coordinates
  • IBO directory for IB authorisation status (CIS, RP Goenka confirmed)
  • School admission portals (admissiontree.in, AWES ERP, direct school portals)

Secondary Sources

  • Reputable news for attributable competitive‑exam mentions (NEET/JEE) — e.g. DPS Ruby Park JEE Main 2024
  • Parent‑directory platforms (ezyschooling, schoolmykids, schoolwiser) — labelled as non‑official
  • Admission aggregator sites (uniapply, admissiontree) for process / fee estimates

Data Quality Notes

Some school sites require JavaScript rendering and were partially inaccessible to non-JS parsers at the time of access (e.g. DPS Newtown admissions page). In such cases, admissions status is not inferred and remains "Unspecified". One CISCE-listed site URL appeared to show unrelated content — directory relies on CISCE-listed phone/emails and labels the website as requiring verification.

Rules Applied

Admissions: Only Open/Closed/Rolling when explicitly stated; else Unspecified
Fees & ratios: Only when explicitly available; else Unspecified
Outcomes: Only from school/board disclosure or reputable report; else Unspecified
New schools: Cross-verified from 2+ sources before inclusion
